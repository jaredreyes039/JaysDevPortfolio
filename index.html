ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Jay, and I'm a fresh face in the front-end web development scene with over 1 year of experience coding in html, css, and javascript. Having developed severe agoraphobia in early 2020, I had to find a new way to live my life and feel the same freedom I felt before, and that's when I found coding. Beginning my journey in Python through the FreeCodeBootCamp, I quickly jumped into front-end development taking on HTML, followed by CSS, and finally implementing JS to bring my projects to life. Along my journey I experienced a self-discovery like no-other, and have found the freedom I was missing. Now, I pursue my engineering degree while bringing elegant, functional sites to fruition!
